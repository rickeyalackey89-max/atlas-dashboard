Under the Hood

How Atlas Works

Not a gut feeling. Not a tipster. A real quantitative engine.

1
Monte Carlo Simulation
For every player and every stat, Atlas runs 10,000 game simulations using rolling performance data, matchup context, pace, and injury state.
2
Machine Learning Calibration
A 14-model GBM ensemble trained on tens of thousands of historical legs converts raw probabilities into calibrated, market-aware confidence scores.
3
Optimal Slip Selection
A beam-search optimizer assembles the best 3, 4, and 5-leg combinations balancing expected value, hit probability, and team diversity.
