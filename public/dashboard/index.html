Betting BasicsFree
📊
What is a Parlay?

A parlay combines multiple picks into one slip. All legs must hit for you to win. Higher risk, higher payout. Atlas optimizes the leg selection so you play the highest-edge combinations.

⚖️
What is Expected Value?

EV measures the long-run return of a bet. A positive EV pick means the true probability of hitting exceeds what the market is pricing in. Atlas finds these edges daily.

🎯
Over vs Under

Over means a player exceeds a stat line. Under means they fall below it. Atlas scores both directions independently — some UNDER picks carry just as much edge as OVERs.

📈
What is a Line?

The line is the threshold set by the sportsbook — e.g. "23.5 points." Atlas compares each player's simulated distribution against that line to find where the book is wrong.

👳
Bankroll Management

Never bet more than you can afford to lose. A flat-unit approach — same stake per slip — is the most consistent way to grow over time. The edge compounds. Blown bankrolls don't.

🔢
What is Confidence?

Atlas confidence is the calibrated probability that a leg hits, expressed as a percentage. 80%+ is high. These are legs the model sees a strong signal on relative to the market line.
