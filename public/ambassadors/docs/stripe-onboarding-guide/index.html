Stripe Onboarding Guide

This guide helps approved AtlasSportsAI Ambassadors complete secure Stripe Express payout onboarding. Stripe Express is where payout identity, banking, tax identity, and 1099 reporting data are submitted securely. Atlas Ambassador agreement and conduct documents are signed separately before Stripe opens.

Process Overview

  1. Submit a quick application or receive an invite from Atlas.
  2. If approved, open the temporary Atlas onboarding link.
  3. Read the Ambassador documents and sign the Atlas Ambassador agreement.
  4. Create your Atlas portal credentials.
  5. Continue to Stripe Express payout, banking, tax identity, and 1099 reporting setup.
  6. Submit all required Stripe Express information.
  7. Atlas portal access activates after both Atlas agreement signing and Stripe Express onboarding are complete.

Before You Start

  • Use the same email address you used for Atlas Ambassador onboarding unless Atlas tells you otherwise.
  • Have your legal name, address, tax information, and payout account details ready.
  • Have a public profile or website ready for the place where you promote Atlas.
  • Complete the Atlas agreement signature step before entering Stripe Express.
  • Use accurate information. Stripe controls verification and may request more details.

What Stripe Express Handles

  • Payout identity verification.
  • Banking or debit-card payout details.
  • Tax identity information Stripe needs for 1099 reporting.
  • Additional verification if Stripe requires it.

Atlas does not collect or store your bank account, SSN, tax ID, or identity document on the Atlas site. Submit those details only through Stripe Express.

What Atlas Handles Separately

  • Ambassador agreement acceptance.
  • Conduct requirements and responsible-use language.
  • Media guide and promotional rules.
  • Payout terms, program launch approval, and portal access rules.

Portal access should unlock only after the Atlas agreement is signed and Stripe Express reports payout/tax onboarding complete.

Website Or Public Profile

When Stripe asks for a website, enter your own public promotion location. This can be a TikTok profile, YouTube channel, Instagram profile, X profile, Twitch profile, Linktree, Discord landing page, or personal website.

Do not use https://atlassports.ai unless Atlas specifically tells you to. AtlasSportsAI is the platform. The Stripe Express connected account belongs to the Ambassador or payee completing payout onboarding.

Product Description

Use a plain description of your Ambassador activity. Example:

I promote AtlasSportsAI subscription services as an independent Ambassador through social media and referral content.

Do not describe yourself as a sportsbook, casino, bookmaker, or gambling operator unless that is actually your separate business. AtlasSportsAI sells statistical analysis and informational subscriptions.

Industry And Business Type

  • Choose the closest accurate Stripe industry/category for your activity.
  • In most cases, your Ambassador activity should align with marketing, advertising, creator, referral, content, or online services if those options are available.
  • Choose the business type that accurately matches who receives payouts and files tax documents: individual, sole proprietor, company, or other entity.
  • If unsure, contact support@atlassports.ai.

Payout And Tax Information

Stripe Express may ask for date of birth, address, tax classification, identity information, and bank or debit-card payout details.

Atlas does not collect or store your bank account, SSN, or tax ID on the Atlas site. Submit those details only through Stripe.

After Submission

  • Return to the Atlas Ambassador portal.
  • Log in with the email and password created during Atlas onboarding.
  • Portal access activates after Atlas agreement signing and Stripe Express payout/tax onboarding are both complete.
  • If Stripe still shows incomplete requirements, finish the missing items from Stripe.

Support

For questions, email support@atlassports.ai from the email address connected to your Ambassador account.
