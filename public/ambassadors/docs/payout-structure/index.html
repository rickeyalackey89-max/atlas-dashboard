Payout Structure

This document defines the launch payout model for approved AtlasSportsAI Ambassadors. Payouts require approved Ambassador status, valid paid customer signups, completed secure payout onboarding, and compliance with the conduct requirements.

Customer Signup Payouts

Signup TypeAmbassador PayoutValidation Rule
Monthly customer$5 one-time payoutValid paid customer after trial conversion and review.
Annual customer$25 one-time payoutValid paid annual customer with tracked Ambassador attribution.

A signup is not valid if it is refunded, charged back, fraudulent, duplicate, self-referral abuse, or otherwise rejected during Atlas review.

Sponsor Payouts

Ambassadors may invite other Ambassadors. Sponsor payouts are earned only when a personally sponsored Ambassador produces a valid paid customer sale. Nobody is paid for recruiting alone.

Sponsored Ambassador SaleSponsor PayoutCondition
Monthly customer sale$5Sale must be valid and attributed to the sponsored Ambassador.
Annual customer sale$25Sale must be valid and attributed to the sponsored Ambassador.

Access Rules

  • Approved Ambassadors receive premium site access in 7-day windows.
  • Every 2 valid paid customer signups renews access for another 7-day period.
  • Access can pause if the renewal threshold is not met.
  • Access is separate from payout eligibility and can be removed for conduct violations.

Payout Timing

  • Payouts require completed secure direct-deposit and tax onboarding.
  • Payouts may be held during refund, chargeback, fraud, or eligibility review.
  • Atlas may adjust or reverse payouts tied to invalid or reversed customer payments.
  • Final payout timing is governed by the written Ambassador agreement and payout provider availability.
