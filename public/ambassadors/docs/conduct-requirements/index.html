Required Policy

Conduct Requirements

Approved AtlasSportsAI Ambassadors represent the brand in public. These rules define required behavior, prohibited claims, brand conduct, and removal triggers.

Core Standards

  • Promote AtlasSportsAI honestly, professionally, and without misleading claims.
  • Do not claim Atlas guarantees wins, profit, income, or betting outcomes.
  • Do not present model outputs as financial, legal, gambling, or investment advice.
  • Respect responsible-gambling standards and do not target minors.

Prohibited Claims

  • No guaranteed profit, risk-free, can't-miss, or guaranteed-outcome language.
  • No fake screenshots, fake earnings, fake customer results, or fabricated testimonials.
  • No claims that Atlas is affiliated with PrizePicks, DraftKings, FanDuel, the NBA, MLB, or any sportsbook/operator unless authorized in writing.
  • No instructions encouraging users to gamble beyond their means.
  • No harassment, hate speech, threats, impersonation, spam, or platform abuse.

Creator confidence language such as "my lock of the day" is allowed when it is clearly personal excitement or opinion, not a promise that Atlas guarantees the outcome.

Brand And Media Conduct

  • Use the Atlas name and visuals accurately.
  • Do not alter logos in ways that misrepresent the brand.
  • Do not imply you are an employee, owner, or official spokesperson unless Atlas authorizes it.
  • Do not collect payment, resell access, or sell Atlas content outside AtlasSportsAI checkout, the Atlas dashboard, official Atlas Discord/Premium areas, Atlas-issued Stripe checkout links, or Atlas-approved Ambassador codes/links.
  • Do not leak premium content or private operational information.

Removal Triggers

Atlas may suspend access, cancel payout eligibility, remove an Ambassador from the program, or reject signups tied to:

  • Fraudulent signups, chargeback abuse, duplicate account abuse, or self-referral manipulation.
  • Repeated misleading marketing or prohibited claims.
  • Violation of platform rules on TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, X, Discord, or other channels.
  • Conduct that harms Atlas users, AtlasSportsAI, or the integrity of the program.
