Atlas Ambassador Agreement

This agreement summarizes the required operating terms for approved AtlasSportsAI Ambassadors. Ambassadors sign this agreement during Atlas onboarding before completing Stripe Express payout setup and receiving active portal access.

Independent Ambassador Status

  • Ambassadors participate as independent promoters, not employees, owners, agents, or legal representatives of AtlasSportsAI.
  • Ambassadors may not bind AtlasSportsAI to contracts, promises, guarantees, refunds, payment terms, or customer commitments.
  • Participation does not create guaranteed income, guaranteed work, guaranteed access, or guaranteed future employment.

Program Documents

The following documents are part of the Ambassador operating rules and should be reviewed before signing:

  • Payout Structure
  • Conduct Requirements
  • Media Guide
  • Opportunity Overview
  • Stripe Express Onboarding Guide

Atlas may update program language before full launch approval. Current documents in the paperwork library are the source of truth.

Payout Eligibility

  • Payouts require approved Ambassador status, tracked attribution, valid paid customer signups, and completed Stripe Express payout onboarding.
  • A customer signup is not valid if it is refunded, charged back, fraudulent, duplicate, self-referral abuse, or otherwise rejected during Atlas review.
  • Sponsor payouts are single-level only and are earned only when a personally sponsored Ambassador produces a valid paid customer sale.
  • Nobody is paid for recruiting alone.

Media And Conduct

  • Ambassadors may create their own graphics, videos, scripts, thumbnails, captions, livestreams, and sales creatives.
  • Ambassadors must not make guaranteed-outcome claims, fake performance claims, fake balance claims, or misleading income claims.
  • Ambassadors must not target minors, promote irresponsible gambling behavior, leak private operational information, or misuse Atlas branding.
  • Atlas may suspend access, reject signups, or remove an Ambassador for conduct that harms users, AtlasSportsAI, or the integrity of the program.

Stripe Express And Tax Identity

  • Stripe Express handles payout identity, direct deposit, banking, tax identity, and future 1099 tax form access.
  • Atlas does not collect bank account information, SSNs, tax IDs, or identity documents through the public Atlas site.
  • Ambassadors must complete Stripe Express payout onboarding before portal access and payout eligibility are finalized.

Portal Access

  • Approved Ambassadors receive site access in 7-day windows.
  • Every 2 valid paid customer signups renews access for another 7-day period.
  • Access may pause if the renewal threshold is not met or if conduct review is required.
  • Portal access is separate from payout eligibility.

Signature Record

By signing during Atlas onboarding, the Ambassador confirms they have reviewed the Ambassador documents, agree to follow the current program rules, understand that Stripe Express separately handles payout and tax identity data, and authorize Atlas to store the digital signature record.
