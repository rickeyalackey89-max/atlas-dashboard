Onboarding Flow

Approved Ambassadors should complete these steps before promotion and payouts begin.

1. Approval

Atlas confirms your Ambassador status, code, sponsor relationship if any, and portal login access.

2. Documents

Review the payout schedule, conduct requirements, media guide, and opportunity overview below.

3. Secure Payout

Complete direct-deposit and tax onboarding only through the secure payout flow after approval.

4. Launch

Use your code, follow the media rules, track valid signups, and maintain site access through sales activity.
