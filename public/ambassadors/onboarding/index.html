Onboarding Flow

Approved Ambassadors should complete these steps before promotion and payouts begin.

1. Approval

Atlas confirms your Ambassador status, code, sponsor relationship if any, and portal login access.

2. Atlas Agreement

Review the payout schedule, conduct requirements, media guide, opportunity overview, and sign the Atlas Ambassador agreement.

3. Stripe Express

Complete payout identity, banking, and required tax reporting setup only through Stripe Express.

4. Portal Access

Portal access unlocks after Atlas agreement signing and Stripe Express onboarding are both complete.

Final payout eligibility requires approved status, valid paid customer signups, completed secure payout setup, and no refund or chargeback disqualification.

Single-level sponsor rule: Atlas Ambassador sponsor payouts are not pyramid or multi-generation compensation. A personally sponsored Ambassador can create a sponsor payout when they produce a valid paid customer sale, but their downstream Ambassadors do not create additional 3rd-generation, 4th-generation, or deeper branch payouts for the original sponsor. Atlas may offer separate team competitions or milestone bonuses when total team thresholds are met.

Payout Country

Enter the two-letter country code for the person or business receiving payouts. Non-US Ambassadors should not select US unless they are legally onboarding as a US payee with required US tax information. If Stripe Express cannot support the selected country, Atlas support will review the payout path manually.

International payout review
Stripe Express is not available for the selected payout country. Enter the PayPal email that should receive payouts. Atlas will manually review the profile before releasing payouts.
