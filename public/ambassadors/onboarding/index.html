Complete Your Ambassador Onboarding

Use this page when you were referred, answered an Atlas ad, or are ready to move beyond the quick application. Atlas reviews onboarding before promo codes, portal access, payout setup, and tax/direct-deposit steps are activated.

Step 1

Submit your identity, contact info, preferred Ambassador code, login password, sponsor code if referred, and promotion notes.

Step 2

Atlas reviews the onboarding request, confirms the code, and activates the Ambassador portal if approved.

Step 3

Tax forms and direct-deposit setup are handled through secure payout onboarding, not through public website fields.

Program Access And Bonuses

  • Approved Ambassadors receive a promo code, portal login, and site access instructions.
  • Site access starts in 7-day windows. Every 2 valid paid customer signups renews access for another 7 days.
  • The top performer each month receives a $500 bonus.
  • A public leaderboard begins after 20 approved Ambassadors have joined.
  • The first Ambassador to 1,000 valid signups receives an additional $2,500 bonus.
