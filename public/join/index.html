What We Are Building

The Atlas Roadmap

The goal is a full sports analytics ecosystem: models, dashboards, AI workflow, creator distribution, and eventually a dedicated app experience.

AtlasGPT

An AI bet helper inside the Atlas experience. The goal is fast board explanations, slip review, model reasoning, and plain-English answers. It is analysis support, not financial or gambling advice.

Atlas App

A dedicated app experience for live slips, alerts, model explanations, premium tools, Ambassador tracking, and mobile-first workflows.

All-Sports Expansion

NBA is the working production model. MLB is the next buildout, with NFL, college, soccer, and additional sports on the roadmap as data pipelines mature.

Sobol Engine Upgrade

Atlas is moving toward a Sobol / Quasi-Monte Carlo simulation layer for better coverage, lower simulation noise, and more stable tail estimates.

Market Intelligence

We are integrating live market signals, PrizePicks payout quotes, EV views, injury context, model calibration, and replay auditing into one workflow.

Human + AI Operations

Atlas is being designed for developers, AI agents, operators, creators, and Ambassadors to work around the same model outputs and product roadmap.
