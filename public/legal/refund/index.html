We offer refunds under the following circumstances:

How to Request a Refund

Email us at support@atlassports.ai with your account email and the date of the charge. We will respond within 2 business days.

To cancel your subscription, contact us via the methods above. We do not currently offer a self-service cancellation portal — reach out and we will cancel immediately.
