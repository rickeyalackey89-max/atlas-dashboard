Warning Signs of Problem Gambling

You may have a gambling problem if you:

  • Feel the need to gamble with increasing amounts of money
  • Have repeated unsuccessful efforts to control or stop gambling
  • Feel restless or irritable when trying to cut back
  • Gamble to escape problems or relieve feelings of helplessness or anxiety
  • Lie to family members or others to conceal gambling activity
  • Jeopardize or lost a significant relationship, job, or opportunity because of gambling
  • Rely on others to provide money to relieve a desperate financial situation caused by gambling

Practical Steps

  • Set a strict budget before participating in any daily fantasy or betting activity
  • Only use money you can afford to lose entirely
  • Take regular breaks and never chase losses
  • Use self-exclusion tools offered by gambling and daily fantasy platforms
  • Block gambling sites using tools like Gamban or GAMSTOP
  • Talk to someone you trust if gambling is affecting your life
